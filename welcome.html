04 The method

The Money 7 — seven videos every service business needs before any other content.

These aren't trending hooks. They're the seven specific videos that answer the questions every prospect is already Googling about your industry, in your suburb, right now. Get these on your site and channel — everything else compounds.

09 Honest answers

The questions everyone actually asks before booking.

No marketing-speak. Same answers I give on a sales call.

I'm not on camera. Will this still work for me?

Yes — but I'll be honest, the operators who film themselves outperform the ones who don't, every time. The workshop covers camera fear directly. By Day 1 lunch you'll have filmed your first one. Most people who say "I'm not on camera" walk out with three videos already shot.

How is this different from hiring an agency?

An agency builds something they own. When the contract ends, the system goes with them. I build something you own — your voice, your scripts, your videos, your workbook. If we stop working together tomorrow, your content keeps running.

I've done courses before and never finished them. Why is this different?

This isn't a course. The workshop is two scheduled days where you build the system in real time, with me on the call. There's nothing to finish on your own. You leave with the work done — not a folder of unwatched videos.

How do I know if I should do the session, the workshop, or the advisory?

Session if you want clarity before committing money. Workshop if you want the system built in two days and the confidence to run it. Advisory if you want someone in your corner every week and you'd rather have a CMO than figure it out yourself. Most people start with the session and self-select into one of the other two.

What if I'm in a niche you haven't worked with?

The Money 7 isn't industry-specific — it's prospect-specific. The questions your prospects Google before calling you are structurally the same as the ones every prospect Googles. The answers change. The framework doesn't.

What does "results" actually look like in 90 days?

Realistic baseline: a published library of 10–20 videos, a system your team can run, and a measurable lift in qualified inquiries. Some clients see leads in week three. Some see them in week ten. What's consistent: by 90 days you have something you can point at and say "this is working."

Can I pay in instalments?

Workshop: yes — three payments of $500 + GST. Session: not needed at $350. Advisory: monthly billing on the three-month minimum, then month-to-month, cancel anytime.

What if I book and it's not the right fit?

If we get on the strategy session and I genuinely don't think I'm the right person to help you — I'll say so on the call and refund you. I'd rather lose $350 than have someone in the workshop who shouldn't be there.
